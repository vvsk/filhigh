What do we do

We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, as well as our ability to deliver for our partners.We match TOP TALENT with the world's most innovative companies.

FliHigh was founded in 2013 to help transformative companies flourish in a continually evolving technology marketplace through the successful recruitment of executives and engineers. Our full stack recruiting approach enables us to help people and companies reach their full potential. We take a hands-on approach to everything we do at Flihigh and we understand that our hard work plays a critical role in helping others flourish

How do we do it

Connect

We spend enough time with your hiring manager and team to connect and understand your culture, processes and working styles

Learn

We learn more about your requirement, the type of person you are looking for from a holistic perspective

Map

We go through our talent pool with a fine comb approach to help map the perfect candidates for your team

Collaborate

We collaborate with your team to validate the list of candidates we have chosen from our pool

Harmonize

We work with you and the candidate to set potential expectations and ensure that you find the best person for the job

Recruit

Once you have found the best candidate, we help you with the recruitment formalities
